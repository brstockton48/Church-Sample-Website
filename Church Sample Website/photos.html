Mount Olive Media Gallery

Welcome to the Mount Olive Baptist Church Media Gallery. We here at Mount Olive believe the age old saying that a picture is worth 1,000 words. The same could and should be said about videos. For that reason, we do our best to incorporate the use of media into our services. This would include music videos with words so that our congregation can sing along. In addition, we incorporate the use of Powerpoint and video clips into our messages each and every week. Our reasoning for this is that we believe someone is much more likely to remember and retain information if they have both heard and seen it. It is often easy to forget the words that are said, it is much more difficult to forget an engaging video

While we think the use of media is important and helpful, we also want to be clear that it does not replace the Bible or the importance of joining together as a congregation and singing with the accompaniment of the piano and other instruments. At our church, we have a great balance and strive to make room for both.

Please enjoy our picture slide shows at the top of the page and videos to the right. Pictures include different members of the congregation, VBS photos, building and grounds and God's wonderful creation. Please visit often because these photos and videos will be updated on a regular basis.
